The energy boost
in the morning.

The full-spectrum daylight lamp that can be switched on at the front is a real wake-up call. With an effect of up to 10,000 lux, you reactivate your bodily functions for more vitality, wakefulness and concentration.

Full light. Full focus

The downward desk illumination provides wide-area illumination with linear, shadow-free light. For effortless work with documents or on the monitor.

Sets the scene perfectly.

The switchable, frontal light function acts like a soft box and creates a natural brightening of the area around the face. For a perfect appearance in video conferences!

Outside dark.
Inside atmospheric.

The indirect light of HEAVN One creates an atmospheric ambience and eliminates the cave feeling of typical desk lamps. The comfortable room illumination is easy on the eyes when working on a monitor and enhances well-being and concentration when it is already dark outside.

In the evening, the light from HEAVN One becomes warmer and stimulates the production of the sleep hormone melatonin in your body. The result is healthy sleep - the best foundation for sustainable performance at work.
